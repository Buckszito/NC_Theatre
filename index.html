about



NC Theatre
The Northern Cape Theatre was built as an assembly hall in the early 1950s as part of the then Kimberley Technical College and was known as the Kimberley Theatre. Post 1994 democratic dispensation the Theatre and the College then fell under a new Department of Education, Sport Arts and Culture in the Northern Cape. The Theatre was then renamed Northern Cape Theatre from Kimberley Theatre. Having the Northern Cape Theatre been identified as a 2010 FIFA World Cup Legacy project by the provincial administration, a business plan to upgrade the facility was then developed by the DSAC's 2010 Unit through Arts and Culture Sub-Committee Coordinating office and submitted to national Department of Arts and Culture for infrastructural funding
